Notes on Pāli grammar books
	1. The text of Kaccāyana, Rūpasiddhi, Mukhamattadīpanī, Saddanīti Suttamālā, and Abhidhānappadīpikā are converted from Thai script available at 'Palipage' (https://sites.google.com/view/palipage).
	2. Kaccāyanasuttaniddesa is taken from GRETIL with some adjustments.
	3. Other texts are taken from the Chaṭṭha Saṅgāyana collection with rearrangement and some editing.
	4. Specific information of each book can be found in the viewer.
	5. Only Kaccāyana, Moggallāna, and Abhidhānappadīpikā have related books viewable in corresponding suttas. Try option button when available.
